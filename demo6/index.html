What We have

Wooden Flooring

Lamiwood’s Engineered Flooring is a high-quality Engineered Wood product and is manufactured in 14 to 15mm thickness with several layers of wood. The top layer or wear layer of engineered flooring is the wood species you are buying including Oak or Walnut (Precious Wood) which determines the grains, colour, and the entire look of the floor. Other layers beneath the top layer are made up of hardwood or softwood material in 3 layer or multilayer construction which controls the wood and makes sure that it stays straight.

At Lamiwood laminated wooden Floors nothing matters more than customers enjoying their floors for years to come. Your satisfaction — day after day for years — is what drives us. When considering a laminate flooring in a home for either new construction or an upgrade, we listen carefully to the type of environment a customer wants to create. Lamiwood’s laminated wooden flooring offer impressive authenticity that recreates the look and feel of real hardwood while remaining a great cost-effective solution for every budget.


We also offer Bamboo decking & cladding for outdoor use which is considered as an immensely stable flooring material and a genuinely harvested product which puts no environmental impact in the form of deforestation. Check out our contemporary range of Outdoor Wooden Floorings.


Designed to replicate the natural colour, grain, and texture of real hardwood, stone, and even patterns, Our Waterproof laminate flooring offers the best of both worlds – impeccable style and practical features. This flooring is made up of 12mm strong HDF with a density of more than 1000 kg/m3 and is easy-to-install, easy-to-maintain, and offers the complete peace of mind of waterproof protection.
